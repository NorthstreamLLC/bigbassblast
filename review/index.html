Overview

Big Bass Blast is the latest chapter in Pragmatic Play and Reel Kingdom's wildly successful Big Bass series. Released in 2026, it takes the core fishing slot formula — Fisherman Wilds collecting Money symbols — and turns up the intensity with new blast mechanics, a higher max win, and a more volatile math model.

The game sits on a classic 5×3 grid with 10 fixed paylines, keeping things straightforward while the bonus features do all the heavy lifting. With a 5,000× max win and high volatility, this slot is firmly aimed at players who want big swings and massive payout potential.

Features in Detail

Fisherman Wild & Money Symbols

The Fisherman symbol is the engine of this game. It appears on all reels and substitutes for all regular symbols. More importantly, when a Fisherman lands on a spin that also has Money symbols visible, it collects all Money values on the reels and adds them together for an instant win. Multiple Fisherman symbols on the same spin each collect independently, which is where the massive wins originate.

Money symbols carry random coin values from a fraction of your bet up to several hundred times it. Getting a high-value Money symbol cluster when two or three Fishermen land is the primary path to four and five-figure multiplier wins.

Bazooka & Hook Assist

Two random features can trigger in the base game. The Bazooka blasts additional Money symbols onto the reels, increasing the collection potential of any Fisherman Wilds present. The Hook can drag a Fisherman Wild into position on a reel that didn't originally show one. Both features fire without warning and can turn an ordinary spin into a substantial win.

Free Spins

Three or more Scatter symbols (fishing rods) trigger the Free Spins bonus. Land 3, 4, or 5 Scatters to receive 5, 7, or 9 Free Spins respectively. During the bonus, Fisherman Wilds operate the same way but benefit from accumulating multipliers — these stack up to 10× across the session. Retriggers are unlimited, and each retrigger adds 5 more Free Spins.

The Free Spins round is where the 5,000× max win is realistically achievable, especially with multipliers active and multiple Money symbols being collected simultaneously.

Bonus Buy & Ante Bet

Players can skip base-game waiting and purchase the Free Spins bonus directly for 100× the current bet. A separate Ante Bet option costs 50% extra per spin but increases the frequency of Scatter symbols, shortening the average wait for the bonus round.
