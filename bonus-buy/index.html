Is the Bonus Buy worth it?

The Bonus Buy costs 100× your bet and delivers guaranteed access to the Free Spins bonus. The key question is whether paying a premium for instant access makes financial sense.

The math: At a bonus frequency of 1 in 59 spins, you'd expect to spend roughly 59× your bet reaching the bonus naturally. The Bonus Buy costs 100× — so you're paying approximately 70% more than the base-game "average" path to the bonus.

However, this comparison isn't entirely fair. Natural triggering has high variance — you might hit the bonus in 10 spins or wait 200+. The Bonus Buy eliminates that variance entirely at the cost of a fixed premium.

Same RTP, different experience: The Bonus Buy has the same mathematical RTP as the base game. You're not changing your expected return — you're buying certainty over timing.

Ante Bet vs Bonus Buy

If your goal is simply to reach the bonus round more often, the Ante Bet is almost always better value than the Bonus Buy. At 50% extra per spin, you're paying a moderate premium for higher scatter frequency, and you still benefit from any wins in the base game along the way. The Bonus Buy makes sense mainly when you specifically want to skip base-game play entirely.
