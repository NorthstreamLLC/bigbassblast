What happens during Free Spins

The Free Spins round uses the same Fisherman Wild + Money symbol collection mechanic as the base game, but with a crucial addition: multipliers.

Multiplier system

Each time a Fisherman Wild collects Money symbols during the bonus, a multiplier builds up. This multiplier applies to all subsequent collections and stacks with each activation, reaching up to a maximum of 10×. Once you hit 10×, every collection in the remaining spins benefits from the full multiplier — this is the setup that produces the game's biggest wins.

Retriggers

Landing 3 or more Scatter symbols during the Free Spins round retriggers the bonus, adding 5 more spins. There's no cap on retriggers — you can keep adding spins indefinitely, which extends the window for multipliers to stack.

Tip: The most valuable bonus sessions are ones where you retrigger with a high multiplier already active. Even a modest Money symbol collection at 10× becomes a major win.

Maximizing your Free Spins

Use Ante Bet to trigger faster

The Ante Bet increases your cost per spin by 50% but significantly boosts Scatter frequency. If your bankroll allows it, Ante Bet is often the most efficient way to reach the bonus round more often per session.

Bigger bets, bigger multiplier payoffs

Because Money symbol values are based on your bet size, and the multiplier applies to those values, there's a compounding effect at higher stakes. A 10× multiplier on a $1 bet spin is worth far less than the same multiplier at $5 a spin.

Don't panic-quit

High-volatility bonus rounds don't always start fast. It's normal for the first 2–3 spins to produce small or no collections. The multiplier builds over the session — staying in gives it time to compound.
