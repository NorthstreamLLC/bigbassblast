Key differences

Big Bass Blast introduces the Bazooka and Hook assist mechanics that don't appear in older entries like Big Bass Bonanza. These random base-game features add Money symbols or pull in Fisherman Wilds, creating additional win opportunities outside of the standard payline and collection mechanics.

The multiplier system in Big Bass Blast's Free Spins round (up to 10×, stacking with each collection) makes the bonus round more dynamic and potentially more rewarding than earlier games in the series, depending on how the multiplier builds during a given session.
