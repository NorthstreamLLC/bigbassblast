What makes Big Bass Blast special

An explosive evolution of the fishing slot formula — loaded with new mechanics and massive win potential.

Fisherman Wild

The Fisherman appears on all reels, substitutes for regular symbols, and — most importantly — collects every Money symbol on the reels the moment it lands.

Money Symbols

Cash-value coins scatter across all reels. When a Fisherman lands alongside them, their values combine for an instant win. Multiple Fishermen multiply the effect.

Bazooka Blast

A random base-game feature that fires extra Money symbols onto the reels. When a Fisherman also lands, the added symbols get collected too.

Hook Assist

Randomly drags a Fisherman Wild into position on a reel. Combined with any Money symbols present, this can create surprise wins from a quiet spin.

Free Spins + 10× Multiplier

3–5 Scatters trigger 5–9 Free Spins. A multiplier builds with each collection, stacking up to 10×. Retriggers are unlimited and the multiplier carries over.
